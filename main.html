The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 drama film based on a novella by Stephen King. It follows the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover. Over the course of two decades, he befriends a fellow inmate, Ellis Redding, and they form an unlikely bond as Andy seeks redemption and freedom.

Actors

The movie stars Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne and Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding. Both actors delivered iconic performances that made their characters unforgettable in cinema history.
