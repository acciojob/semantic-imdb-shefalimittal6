Summary

Inception is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for planting an idea in a target's subconscious, a task considered to be impossible.

Actors

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
